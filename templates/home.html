About The Project

AI Teacher Assistant is an educational technology platform built using Django, Python, Gemini AI, NLP, and SQLite. The system helps students transform study materials into summaries, quizzes, and AI-powered learning experiences while tracking academic progress through analytics.

Designed, developed, and deployed as a full-stack AI application integrating document analysis, question answering, quiz generation, performance tracking, and intelligent educational assistance.
